Academic Perspective

My main motivation is to understand how complex computing systems work under the surface. I am drawn to questions about instruction flow, memory behavior, parallel execution, system bottlenecks, and the design trade-offs that shape modern processors and runtime systems.


Rather than viewing software and hardware as separate layers, I am interested in how they influence each other in real systems, especially in high-performance and data-intensive workloads.
